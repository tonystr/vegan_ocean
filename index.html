Eat green to save the blue

UN SDG 14

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goal #14, or UNSDG14 for short, is an international goal to preserve life below water. Although you've probably heard about how plastic is killing sea life, we're contributing to their decimation by eating them too. Let's stop relying on ending lives to pleasure our taste buds, and focus on delicious foods from plants instead!
